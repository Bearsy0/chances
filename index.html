Probability Calculators



DISCLAIMER: I am not responsible for your gambling problems. I checked the math as best as I could. This site is easier to use on desktops.

The calculators implemented here are potentially useful for estimating probabilities in gacha games or similar scenarios:

  • Chance Calculator: Estimate the probability of drawing items a number of items, when the probability of success is known.
  • Hidden Probability Calculator: Estimate the success probability based on observed successes and failures. (Bayesian statistics derivation)
